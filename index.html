What is Inclusity™?

Inclusity is where inclusion and diversity meet — to help build productive and collaborative environment in which all individuals are valued for their unique contributions.

Inclusion Comes First

After 20 years working in the diversity training space, Maria Arcocha White realized diversity efforts still focused on outward diversity and checking boxes. There was a lack of focus on true culture change and valuing all diversity (whether you could see it or not). In 2013, she founded Inclusity™, to pursue an empathetic, inclusion-focused approach to inclusion and diversity work.

When you build and sustain a workplace culture that truly includes and values each person, then diversity can thrive there. Moreover, inclusion improves your business’ bottom line by increasing employee engagement and productivity.
